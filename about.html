Weronika Sobecka

Artist based in Warsaw, Poland

Weronika Sobecka (born in 1993 in Gostynin) artist, photographer and painter based in Warsaw, Poland. She attended universities in New York, Shanghai and Warsaw and recently graduated from Akademia Fotografii in Warsaw.

“In my art I focus on emotions and relieving the moment. Art enables me to deconstruct reality in order to grasp it, hold on to it for a moment and finally let it go. My aim is to share my personal vulnerability and portray subjective interpretation of human relations.”
